## The recruitment process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply">
    Applicants create an account with their UMD email, fill out the application form, and select the roles they're interested in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    Submitted applications are assigned to reviewers who score them using a structured rubric. Qualified applicants advance to interviews.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Interview">
    Interviewers conduct and score interviews for qualified applicants. Scores are combined with application scores for final ranking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Decision">
    Admins release acceptance decisions. Applicants receive decision letters and can confirm their acceptance through the portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Registration is restricted to email addresses ending in `umd.edu` or `@hack4impact.org`. If you have trouble accessing the portal, contact your club administrator.
</Note>
