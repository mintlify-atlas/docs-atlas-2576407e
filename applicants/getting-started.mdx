The Application Portal is open to any student with a `umd.edu` email address, as well as current club members using their `hack4impact.org` address. Before you can fill out an application, you need to create an account and verify your email. This page walks you through everything you need to do before the form appears.

## Sign up and log in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the portal and click Sign up">
    Navigate to the Application Portal. On the login screen, choose to create a new account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Your email must end in `umd.edu` or `@hack4impact.org`. Accounts created with any other domain will not be accepted by the system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a password and submit">
    Choose a strong password and complete the sign-up form. The portal creates your applicant account immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email address">
    The portal sends a verification message to the address you provided. Open that email and click the verification link before trying to log in. Without verification, you will not be able to access the application.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Return to the portal and sign in with your email and password. After a successful login you are taken to the **Overview** page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The application overview page

Once you are logged in, the **Overview** page shows you the active application for the current semester. From here you can see:

- The **semester** the application is for (for example, *Fall 2025*).
- The **due date and time**, displayed in your local timezone.
- A description of the application and the recruitment process.
- A button to **Apply**, **Continue Application**, or **Go to status page** depending on where you are in the process.

<Info>
  If no active application form exists at the time you log in, the Overview page displays "There are no active forms at the moment." Check back closer to the recruitment period or contact your club administrator.
</Info>

When the deadline has passed and you have not yet submitted, the button changes to **Application Closed** and is disabled. Submitted applicants can still access the status page after the deadline.
